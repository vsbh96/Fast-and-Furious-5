Mexican Cuisine Dessert Sopapillas

Mexican Cuisine


Sopapillas Authentic Recipe is here

Ingredients required:


  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powderr
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable shortening
  • 3/4 cup warm water
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • Powdered sugar and/or honey for serving

Instructions:


  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, baking powder, and salt.Cut in the shortening using a pastry cutter or your fingers until the mixture is crumbly.
  2. Slowly add the warm water to the mixture, stirring until a soft and pliable dough forms
  3. Knead the dough for a few minutes on a floured surface until it is smooth.
  4. Cover the dough with a damp cloth and let it rest for 10-15 minutes.
  5. Heat about 2 inches of vegetable oil in a deep saucepan or frying pan over medium-high heat
  6. Divide the dough into 8-10 equal portions and roll each portion into a ball. Roll each ball out on a floured surface until it is about 1/8 inch thick.
  7. Cut each rolled-out dough into small squares or triangles.Fry the sopapillas in the hot oil until they are golden brown on both sides..
  8. Drain the sopapillas on a paper towel-lined plate to remove any excess oil.
  9. Serve hot with powdered sugar and/or honey for dipping.

Enjoy your delicious and authentic Mexican cuisine dessert!
