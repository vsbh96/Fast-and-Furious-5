Japanese Cuisines

Japanese Teriyaki Salmon


Teriyaki salmon is a popular dish in Japanese cuisine that features salmon fillets that are marinated in a sweet and savory sauce, then grilled or broiled until the skin is crispy and the flesh is tender and flaky.

Tempura


Tempura is a popular Japanese dish that features battered and deep-fried seafood, vegetables.Thin slices or strips of vegetables or seafood are dipped in the batter, then briefly deep-fried in hot oil.
