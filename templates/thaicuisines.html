Thai Cuisines

Pad Thai Authentic


Pad Thai is a popular Thai dish that consists of stir-fried rice noodles, eggs, vegetables, and often meat or seafood, seasoned with a sweet and sour sauce. It is a popular street food in Thailand and has gained popularity around the world.

Pad See Ew


Pad See Ew is a popular Thai noodle dish that is typically made with wide, flat rice noodles stir-fried with soy sauce, garlic, vegetables, and often meat such as chicken, beef or pork. The dish is flavored with sweet soy sauce
