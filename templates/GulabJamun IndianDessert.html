Indian Cuisine Dessert


Gulab Jamun Authentic Recipe is here

Ingredients required:


  • Milk
  • powder
  • All-purpose flour
  • Baking powder
  • Ghee
  • Butter Milk and Sugar Water
  • Cardamom powder and Rosewater

Instructions:


  1. In a bowl, combine 1 cup of milk powder, 1/4 cup of all-purpose flour, and 1/4 cup of fine semolina. Add a pinch of baking soda and mix well.
  2. Gradually add 1/4 cup of whole milk to the dry mixture, and gently mix until you form a soft, smooth dough.
  3. Meanwhile, prepare the sugar syrup by combining 1.5 cups of granulated sugar, 1.5 cups of water, a few crushed cardamom pods, and 1 teaspoon of rose water in a saucepan
  4. Boil the mixture until the sugar dissolves, and then simmer for 5 minutes. Remove from heat and keep the syrup warm.
  5. Divide the dough into small, equal portions and roll them into smooth balls without any cracks.
  6. Heat oil or ghee in a deep frying pan over low heat. Fry the balls in batches, stirring gently to ensure even browning.
  7. Once they are golden brown, remove them with a slotted spoon and drain on a paper towel.
  8. Soak the fried balls in the warm sugar syrup for at least 1 hour, allowing them to absorb the syrup and become soft and spongy.

Enjoy your homemade, delicious and authentic Gulab Jamun Indian Dessert!
