Chinese Cuisine KungPao Chicken

Chinese Cuisine


KungPao Chicken Authentic Recipe is here

Ingredients required:


  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into small pieces
  • 1/2 cup roasted peanuts
  • 4-5 dried red chili peppers
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Chinese cooking wine or dry sherry
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Green onions, chopped for garnish

Instructions:


  1. In a bowl, mix together the chicken pieces with the marinade ingredients. Let marinate for at least 30 minutes in the refrigerator.
  2. In a small bowl, mix together the soy sauce, Chinese cooking wine, cornstarch, and water. Set aside.
  3. Heat a wok or large skillet over high heat. Add the vegetable oil and swirl to coat the pan.
  4. Add the dried red chili peppers and stir-fry for about 30 seconds until fragrant.
  5. Add the chicken and stir-fry for about 3-4 minutes until browned and cooked through.
  6. Add the garlic and ginger and stir-fry for another 30 seconds.
  7. Add the roasted peanuts and stir-fry for another minute.
  8. Pour in the soy sauce mixture and stir-fry for another minute or until the sauce thickens.
  9. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  10. Garnish with chopped green onions and serve hot with steamed rice.

Enjoy your delicious and authentic Chinese cuisine food item!
