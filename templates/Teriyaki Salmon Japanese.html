Japanese Cuisine Teriyaki Salmon

Japanese Cuisine


Teriyaki Salmon Authentic Recipe is here

Ingredients required:


  • 4 salmon fillets (about 6 ounces each)
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons mirin
  • 2 tablespoons sake
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 1 green onion, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions:


  1. In a small saucepan, combine the soy sauce, mirin, sake, brown sugar, vegetable oil, honey, garlic, and ginger. Bring to a boil over medium heat and then reduce the heat to low. Simmer for 5-7 minutes or until the sauce thickens.
  2. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  3. Season the salmon fillets with salt and pepper on both sides.
  4. Heat a large skillet over high heat. Add a tablespoon of vegetable oil to the skillet.
  5. Sear the salmon fillets, skin-side down, for about 2-3 minutes until the skin is crispy and golden brown.
  6. Brush the top of the salmon fillets with the teriyaki sauce.
  7. Transfer the skillet to the preheated oven and bake for another 5-7 minutes, or until the salmon is cooked through.
  8. Remove the skillet from the oven and brush the salmon fillets with more teriyaki sauce.
  9. Garnish with chopped green onions and serve hot with steamed rice.
