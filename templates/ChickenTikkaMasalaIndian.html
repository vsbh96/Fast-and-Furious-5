Indian Cuisine Chicken Tikka Masala

Indian Cuisine


Chicken Tikka Masala Authentic Recipe is here

Ingredients required:


  • 1 pound boneless chicken breast, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • Salt, to taste
  • 3 tablespoons ghee or vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons grated fresh ginger
  • 1 can (14.5 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions:


  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the yogurt, lemon juice, cumin, paprika, garam masala, turmeric, cayenne pepper, and salt to taste. Add the chicken and toss to coat. Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes, or up to 2 hours.
  2. Preheat the oven to 425°F. Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil and lightly grease it. Thread the marinated chicken pieces onto skewers and place them on the prepared baking sheet. Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and slightly charred.
  3. Meanwhile, in a large skillet or saucepan, heat the ghee or vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the onion and sauté until softened and lightly browned, about 5-7 minutes. Add the garlic and ginger and cook for another 1-2 minutes.
  4. Add the crushed tomatoes and stir to combine. Reduce the heat to low and simmer for 10-15 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened and the flavors have melded together.
  5. Stir in the heavy cream and continue to simmer for another 5 minutes, or until the sauce is heated through.
  6. Once the chicken is done, remove the skewers and add the chicken to the sauce. Stir to coat the chicken with the sauce and continue to cook for another 5-10 minutes, or until the chicken is fully coated in the sauce and heated through.
  7. Serve the Chicken Tikka Masala over rice, garnished with fresh cilantro.
