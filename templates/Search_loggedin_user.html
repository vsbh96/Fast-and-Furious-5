Food: KungPao Chicken
Ingredients: 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into small pieces, 1/2 cup roasted peanuts,4-5 dried red chili peppers, 2 cloves garlic, minced,1 teaspoon grated ginger
Instructions: Coat chicken with cornstarch and salt, sauté with bell pepper and peanuts, then add a flavorful mixture of soy sauce, rice vinegar, hoisin sauce, sesame oil, and chili flakes.

Food: Spaghetti Carbonara
Ingredients: 1 pound (450g) spaghetti, 6 ounces (170g) guanciale (cured pork jowl), pancetta (Italian bacon), 4 large egg yolks, 1 cup (100g) freshly grated Pecorino Romano cheese
Instructions: Cook spaghetti according to package instructions, sauté diced bacon and garlic until crispy, then toss with beaten eggs and grated parmesan.

Food: Teriyaki Salmon
Ingredients: 4 salmon fillets (about 6 ounces each), 2 tablespoons soy sauce, 2 tablespoons mirin, 2 tablespoons sake, 2 tablespoons brown sugar, 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
Instructions: Whisk together soy sauce, brown sugar, honey, garlic, and ginger for the teriyaki sauce, then sear salmon fillets in a hot skillet, and broiling until caramelized, and serve.

Food: Enchiladas Verdes
Ingredients: 12 corn tortillas, 1 pound (450g) boneless, skinless chicken breasts, 2 cups (480ml) chicken broth, 1 pound (450g) tomatillos, husked and rinsed,1 small white onion
Instructions: Mix shredded chicken with salsa verde and cilantro, roll tightly in corn tortillas, sprinkle with Monterey Jack cheese and bake until melted, then serve with sour cream.

Food: Mapo Tofu

Food: Gulab Jamun
Ingredients: Milk powder, All-purpose flour, Baking powder, Ghee, Butter Milk and Sugar Water, Cardamom powder and Rosewater
Instructions: Mix together milk powder and flour, add in ghee and enough milk to form a soft dough. Divide the dough into small balls and deep fry until golden brown, then soak in a sugar syrup for a few hours before serving.

Food: Choclate-and-Pistachio Biscotti
Ingredients: 2 cups all-purpose flour, 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder, 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder, 1 cup granulated sugar, 1 teaspoon vanilla extract, Eggs, dark chocolate chips
Instructions: Mix ingredients to form a dough with chopped pistachios and chocolate chips, Bake and slice into biscotti, then bake again until crisp.

Food: Khao-Niaow-Ma-Muang-Mango
Ingredients: 1 1/2 cups glutinous rice, 1 1/2 cups water, 1/4 cup granulated sugar, 1/4 teaspoon salt, 2 ripe mangoes, peeled and sliced, Sesame seeds and/or mint leaves for garnish
Instructions: Soak sticky rice for at least 4 hours, then steam until cooked. Heat coconut milk, sugar, and salt, then pour over the cooked rice and serve with sliced mango.
