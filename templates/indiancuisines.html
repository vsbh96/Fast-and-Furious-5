Indian Cuisines

Chicken Tikka Masala


Chicken tikka masala is a popular dish that originated in the Indian subcontinent.It consists of marinated pieces of chicken that are grilled or baked and then cooked in a spiced tomato-based sauce.

Paneer Butter Masala


Paneer butter masala is a popular North Indian dish that is made with paneer (Indian cottage cheese) cooked in a creamy tomato-based gravy.The dish is usually served with rice, naan bread, or other Indian flatbreads.
