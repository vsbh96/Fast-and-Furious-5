Chinese Cuisines

Kung-Pao-Chicken


Kung Pao Chicken is a classic Chinese dish that is popular both in China and in many other parts of the world. It's a spicy stir-fry made with marinated chicken, peanuts, vegetables, and chili peppers.

Chuan


Chuan is a style of Chinese cuisine that originated in the Sichuan province of southwestern China. It is known for its bold and spicy flavors, with dishes often featuring a combination of hot and numbing flavors from Sichuan peppercorns, chili peppers, and other spices.
